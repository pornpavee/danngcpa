Welcome to Daniel Ng, PLLC Account

Our company is a complete services accounting and consulting company. We offer our services to a large range of customers ranging from individuals to medium-sized companies. We provides complete business services to our clients from setting up sole proprietor company, C-Corporation, S-Corporation, and Partnership, LLC; helping apply restaurant, laundry, appearance enhancement and liquor licenses, etc.; establishing accounting system; selecting business software; preparing financial statements, audit report; filing Corp- tax, payroll tax, sales tax, individual income tax; and handling business purchase and sold, exchanging, and dissolution.

We realize that choosing a qualified CPA is a very important decision for any prospective client. Your accountant has frequent access to some of your most private and important information. A good working relationship with your accountant as a trusted advisor is mandatory. No website or list of credentials can be an adequate substitute for a face to face in-depth meeting to analyze and evaluate your business needs and develop a mutual comfort level.

We will answer all of your questions, as they impact both your tax and financial situations. We welcome you to contact us anytime. I would enjoy the opportunity to meet with you at your business to discuss how my firm can assist you.
